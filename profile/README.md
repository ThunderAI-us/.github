<h1 align="center">ThunderAI</h1>

<p align="center"><strong>Personal AI agents on your own hardware.</strong><br>
Your agent. Your hardware. Your authority.</p>

---

ThunderAI builds agents that live on machines *you* control — not chatbots rented from
someone else's cloud. An agent that remembers your world, holds your credentials in an
encrypted vault, and actually gets things done on your behalf: browse, email, schedule,
watch, act — all under your authority, with a receipt for everything it does.

## The family

- **ThunderGate** — the personal agent runtime. The foundation.
- **ThunderCommo** — agent-to-human communication. The window; ThunderGate is the house.
- **ThunderAgent** — your agent on your phone: calls, voicemail, texts, email.
- **ThunderVault** — secure, end-to-end-encrypted vault control from your pocket.

Built on the **Loop Protocol** trust framework — BYOAA and KYA — for delegation that is
faithful, attributed, and auditable. The trust framework is theirs, the implementation is
ours, and the authority is always yours.
